THE FUTURE OF DATA MINING

Svandis will allow users to participate in data crowdsourcing and receive SVN rewards via the cross-platform DataMining App, an open-source crawling application using language processing and machine learning to analyse information.

DataMining App will harvest and index data sources, enabling the analysis of a vast quantity of historical and real-time data. This automated process will increase the speed and accuracy of the Svandis Newsfeed and Catalyst Analysis tools.

DATA MINING ARCHITECTURE

Data Mining Workers
send crawled links to the worker-api server for validation and then forward the data to the backend content-extractor, which integrates pattern recognition in improve the quality of feed curation.
Machine Learning and Sentiment Analysis
are used to pinpoint catalysts and facilitate a positive feedback loop between the internal analytics team and the Research Community.
Svandis Database
is built upon PostgreSQL and Cassandra databases to ensure agility. PostgreSQL is used for storage of structured information whereas Cassandra is used for cases where a large volume of incoming data is involved.

THE DATAMINING APP PROCESS

1

Svandis User install and runs the DataMining App on their device

2

Mined data is encrypted

3

Rewarded process is triggered: SVN Tokens are sent to the user for contributing

4

Mined data is sent to Svandis

5

Machine Learning algorithms are used to classify and analyse mined data

6

Svandis Community and Analytics Team reviews and validates data

7

Svandis Community and Analytics Team convert the data into valuable information

8

Valuable information is processed and used by each Svandis feature accordingly

RESEARCH COMMUNITY

Svandis approach to data gathering starts with a decentralized Research Community of users dedicated to curating accurate content. Thanks to unique rewarding mechanism of Research Community, each participant will be motivated to find any necessary information, which can be applied in different use cases and industries.

A gamified platform that includes levels, milestones and achievements will ensure that the most reputable and trustworthy actors have the most weight in the system.

The Research Community not only makes the process of data accumulation faster and more efficient, but also brings much-needed objectivity and reliability to the information. Scaling user participation will allow to create an autonomous environment to compile cryptocurrency news and data.

FAQ

What is Svandis?

Svandis is a fintech company that is developing a research platform providing market-leading analytical and visualisation tools for professionals working in crypto market space. Svandis data mining software is applicable to another industries as well, such as global markets, sports and arts.

What does Svandis mean?

Svandis is an Icelandic girl’s name, incorporating the words for swan and fairy.

What is the Svandis Research Platform?

The Svandis Research Platform is a platform for digital assets traders and investors, as well as for other active crypto market participants. Svandis will include the following elements:

  • ICO and token-sale screener
  • Cryptocurrency and token screener
  • Newsfeed
  • Charts
  • Catalysts analysis
  • Heatmaps and Groups
  • Watchlists and Alerts
  • Portfolio
  • Customised user interface
  • Svandis DataMining App
  • Certified reviewer
  • Data analysis
  • Marketplace
  • Svandis indices
  • Indices builder

How can I participate in the Svandis Community?

The main role of the Svandis Community is crowdsourcing. The power of the community is unparalleled and will exponentially increase the capability to detect opportunities or report organised information quickly and efficiently.

The Svandis Community, along with other partners, will receive Svandis tokens (SVN) in recognition of their contribution:

  1. Checking information by using a standardized template and a checklist. The goal is to check the validity of several categories of information published by a source.
  2. Installing a DataMining application. This app will grab website pages and other content to enable the analysis of a vast quantity of historical and real-time data.

What are Svandis tokens?

The token is called Svandis (SVN). It is an utility token that provides access to the Svandis Research Platform. Svandis tokens are ERC-20 compatible tokens distributed on the Ethereum blockchain pursuant to a related ERC-20 smart contract (the “SVN Tokens”). Unlike proof-of-work blockchains such as Bitcoin or Ethereum, there is no mining in Svandis.

What countries are excluded from the token sale?

For legal reasons, we currently cannot accept participants from the US. However, this list of countries may change as regulations in this area change around the world.
