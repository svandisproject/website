Svandis is Hiring !

At Svandis, our mission is to give our users the best trading experience possible. To do that, we depend on the platform, community, and our team of talented, ambitious people who share a common passion for excellence. Whether you have years of experience or are just starting your career in crypto-related services, we’ll help you realize your full potential and achieve your career goals. Other than working with a great team of smart and energetic people, we also offer a very competitive salary. We also have a defined career path for successful individuals in this practice.

Researcher & Analyst

Overview

Svandis wants to create professional opportunities for the most effective and motivated participants of the Svandis community. Those who want to develop their independence while building their career with an ascending company will understand this is the opportunity they were looking for.

Svandis is not “hiring” researchers or analysts. We are looking for partners who want to develop their potential and invest their talent to create value.

Key Responsibilities

  • Prepare insightful reviews on a recurring basis for the Svandis community and internal team (upcoming token sales, worldwide, and political events, industry overview, sector analysis);
  • Provide daily reports over major news for existing tokens, token sales and ICOs;
  • Analysis of trends (ICO market, crypto assets, traditional markets) with the view to provide insight on current situation and trends;
  • Prepare and deliver complex technical information in a clear and engaging style to various type of audience;
  • Update Svandis database by entering/validating data;
  • Participate in the development and implementation of primary and secondary research tools including but not limited to the creation of qualitative research discussion guides, quantitative research survey guides, and the collection and distillation of data from these tools;
  • Keeping track on crypto exchange listings and liquidity;
  • Provide analysis to prioritize addition of new digital assets.

Required Knowledge and Abilities

  • Minimum 3 years’ experience in both crypto and traditional financial markets would be an advantage;
  • The ability to develop a vision and express it to a diversified audience;
  • Having positive leadership and the ability to communicate in a constructive way complex goals;
  • High sense of accountability about your work and the deliverables you decided to accept;
  • High self-motivation;
  • Strong research and analytical background, ability to create innovative solutions to transform data into insightful information;
  • Interpersonal, excellent communication (written and verbal) and presentation skills;
  • Ability to organize schedules and plans in accordance with tight deadline;
  • Acquaintance with crypto space and knowledge of both crypto and traditional financial markets are advantages;
  • Motivated to keep learning.
