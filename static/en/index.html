FAQ

What is Svandis?

Svandis is a fintech company that is developing a research platform providing market-leading analytical and visualisation tools for professionals working in crypto market space.

What does Svandis mean?

Svandis is an Icelandic girl’s name, incorporating the words for swan and fairy.

What is the Svandis Research Platform?

The Svandis Research Platform is a platform for digital assets traders and investors, as well as for other active crypto market participants. Svandis will include the following elements:

  • ICO and token-sale screener
  • Cryptocurrency and token screener
  • Newsfeed
  • Charts
  • Catalysts analysis
  • Heatmaps and Groups
  • Watchlists and Alerts
  • Portfolio
  • Customised user interface
  • Svandis DataMining App
  • Certified reviewer
  • Data analysis
  • Marketplace
  • Svandis indices
  • Indices builder

How can I participate in the Svandis Community?

The main role of the Svandis Community is crowdsourcing. The power of the community is unparalleled and will exponentially increase the capability to detect opportunities or report organised information quickly and efficiently.

The Svandis Community, along with other partners, will receive Svandis tokens (SVN) in recognition of their contribution:

  1. Checking on ICOs and token sales by using a standardized template and a checklist. The goal is to check the validity of several categories of information published by an ICO or token sale.
  2. Installing a DataMining application. This app will grab website pages and other content to enable the analysis of a vast quantity of historical and real-time data.

What are Svandis tokens?

The token is called Svandis (SVN). It is an utility token that provides access to the Svandis Research Platform. Svandis tokens are ERC-20 compatible tokens distributed on the Ethereum blockchain pursuant to a related ERC-20 smart contract (the “SVN Tokens”). Unlike proof-of-work blockchains such as Bitcoin or Ethereum, there is no mining in Svandis.

Is there a pre-sale? When will be the token sale?

The private pre-sale is happening right now, you can apply here. The token sale itself is scheduled for Q3 2018, subscribe to our updates to stay tuned.

What purchase methods will be accepted during the token sale?

Only ETH will be accepted during the token sale.

How can I participate in the Svandis token distribution?

To get involved in the Svandis token distribution, you need an Ethereum wallet compatible with ERC20 tokens. Do not send Ether (“ETH”) directly from cryptocurrency exchanges, use only an ETH compatible wallet.

What countries are excluded from the token sale?

For legal reasons, we currently cannot accept participants from the US. However, this list of countries may change as regulations in this area change around the world.
